Volunteering with UCLA Alumni Association

I am pictured here helping to run a UCLA information booth at San Francisco's Pride 2015 Parade, shortly after the Supreme Court legalized same-sex marriage. With a very strong network in the Bay Area, UCLA's Alumni Association provided a community with social and volunteer events that I attended.

Raytheon Engineering Rotation Program, Class of 2013

I was part of a group of 10 engineers, selected for versatility, ambition and leadership potential. This group was afforded the opportunity to rotate within Raytheon's various business and technical centers. The program provided a larger foundation for leadership and cultivated a community of peers who supported each other, shared knowledge and continuously learned from each other's experiences and rotations.
